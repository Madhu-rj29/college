About Dayananda Sagar University

Dayananda Sagar University (DSU) is a renowned educational institution located in Bangalore, India. Established in 2014, DSU offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines. With state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and industry partnerships, DSU provides a conducive environment for holistic learning and skill development.

Programs Offered

Dayananda Sagar University offers programs in the following fields:

  • Engineering
  • Management
  • Pharmacy
  • Health Sciences
  • Commerce
  • Arts & Humanities
  • Basic & Applied Sciences
  • Design
  • Computer Applications
